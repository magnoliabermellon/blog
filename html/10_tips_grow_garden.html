10 Tips to grow your garden

#1. Put rusty nails in water and spray your plants when the water color turns brown. This generates chlorophyll that helps the photosynthesis process.
#2. Save the eggshells, crush them in the blender, and place the powder on your plants. Egg residues provide calcium.
#3. The banana provides calcium, potassium and phosphorus. Slice the banana and mix it with the earth, my recommendation is to put a little bit since it can attract bugs and insects which can hurt your plants.
#4. If you want to make your compost at home you need vegetable and fruit residues. Lay a layer of soil, and a layer of organic waste. Repeat. And let this mixture ferment for two weeks. It is recommended that it be in an open space as it generates flies and insects. After two weeks it will be ready.
#5. If you have coffee residues (pellets) you can use it on your plants. Coffee gives them potassium, phosphorous, and nitrogen which is good as it helps them grow fast.
#6. Human hair has nitrogen. If you brush your hair and clean the brush, you can use those residues to make it a fertilizer for your plants.
#7. Aspirin in small measures (1/4 of aspirin per 0.94 liters of water) helps to germinate the seeds, it improves the immune system of the plants and makes the flowers bloom.
#8.To kill pests you can create a homemade pesticide. You will use 1 liter of water, 2 tablespoons of baking soda, 1 tablespoon of olive oil, 10 drops of liquid soap. * Remember to check first with a leaf from your plant and if you notice that the pesticide is very strong, dilute it with water. *
#9. Cada planta, vegetal, y fruta tiene su propio cuidado. Para saber que temperatura debe de tener tu planta (su habitat natural) revisa la página: https://www.almanac.com/
#10. Apple cider vinegar at home. Accumulate apple pits (you can freeze them) and when you put several together you can put them in a glass container. Add 4 tablespoons (table spoons) and leave to ferment for 2 weeks. Check the container daily and stir it so it doesn't spoil. At the end of 2 weeks you can strain the product and store it in a glass jar to use it.
