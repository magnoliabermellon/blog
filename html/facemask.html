How to make a facemask at home?

Materials:

1. Facemask pattern (Download it here)

2. Sewing thread.

3. A filter cloth.

4. A fabric for the texture of the face mask.

5. A fabric to repel water.

Instructions:

1. Draw or print the facemask pattern.

2. Sew the materials so it's one piece.

3. Add pins (deja 1cm para la costura).

4. Cut the shape of the facemask (you will have 2 pieces).

5. Sew.

6. You can wear it now.
